Testing your balance and reflexes

There will be two circles on the screen, one large, open circle and one small, solid circle. Keep the smaller circle inside the larger circle by tilting the tablet. There is an initial acceleration applied on the smaller circle and you must over come this acceleration to keep the smaller circle inside the designated boundary. You will be tested on the amount of time the smaller circle is within the boundary.

Press Okay and hold the device flat to begin.
