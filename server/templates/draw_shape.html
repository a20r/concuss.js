Testing your coordination and memory

Two circles will be presented to you for a short period of time. Once the circles disappear, draw line from left to right indicating the line between the two circles. Once you lift your finger, the result will be registered and you will be shown how close you were. This will occur 20 times, each iteration giving you less time to see where the starting and ending circles are. You will be scored on how far your starting and ending points are from the respective circles and on the velocity of your finger on the page.

Press Okay and start drawing the line to begin.
